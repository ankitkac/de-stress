Yoga Poses to De-Stress

Mountain Pose

Mountain Pose improves the posture and the body awareness. Strengthens the core muscles. Tadasana is the foundation of most of the standing asana.

Child's Pose

Child Pose massages the abdominal internal organs, gently stretches the spine hips and thighs, calms the nervous system and relieves tiredness.

Downward Facing Dog

Stretch your hamstrings, calves, and arms. This pose releases tension and energizes the body.

Heron Pose

Heron Pose stretches the ankles, calves, hamstrings, quadriceps, spine and shoulders and stimulate the abdominal.

Monkey pose

Monkey pose improves balance, stability and flexibility of the hips and pelvic girdle. Tones, stretches and strengthens the leg muscles. Enhance the feeling of grounding and balance the nervous system.

Half Standing Forward Bend

Half Standing Forward Bend corrects hyper kyphosis and tones abdominal muscles. Use as a transition pose during the flow.

Tree Pose

Tree Pose helps center yourself and build focus. Calm the mind, improve balance and strengthens the supporting leg.

Eagle Pose

Eagle Pose improves the concentration, calm the mind, stimulate the blood circulation and the immune system.

Cobra Pose

Cobra Pose keeps the spine supple and healthy. Strengthen the back and open the chest encouraging a deeper breath. Promote digestion.
